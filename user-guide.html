Mom's Contact Manager - User Guide

Welcome to your family's secure contact management system! This guide will help you navigate and use all the features effectively.

Getting Started

First Time Setup

  1. Visit the app: https://sadjei78.github.io/ma-webiii
  2. Sign up with your email address and a strong password
  3. Wait for approval - an existing family member will authorize your account
  4. Sign in once you receive confirmation

Sign In

  • Use your email and password to access the app
  • Your session will stay active until you sign out
  • You can access the app from any device with internet

Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is your home screen showing key information:

Quick Stats

  • Total Contacts: See how many contacts you have
  • Important Contacts: Highlighted contacts that need attention
  • Recent Activity: Recently added or updated contacts
  • Categories: Breakdown of contacts by category
Pro Tip

Click any dashboard card to see filtered contacts! This is a quick way to view specific groups of contacts.

Managing Contacts

Adding a New Contact

  1. Click "Add New Contact" button
  2. Fill in the contact information:
    • Name (required)
    • Full Name (optional)
    • Organization (optional)
    • Phone Numbers (click "+" to add multiple)
    • Email Addresses (click "+" to add multiple)
    • Categories (select from dropdown or add new)
    • Notes (any additional information)
  3. Click "Save Contact"

Viewing Contact Details

  • Click any contact card to see full details
  • View all information in a popup dialog
  • Click "Edit" to modify the contact

Contact Status

  • Important: Mark contacts as important (yellow highlight)
  • Archived: Hide contacts from main view (grayed out)
  • Active: Normal contacts (green badge)

Categories & Organization

Using Categories

  • Select categories when adding/editing contacts
  • Multiple categories can be assigned to one contact
  • Filter contacts by category using the dropdown

Managing Categories

  1. Click "Manage Categories" in the dropdown menu
  2. Add new categories for your family's needs
  3. Delete unused categories (contacts will be moved to "Uncategorized")
  4. View all existing categories in the list

Default Categories

  • Family: Immediate and extended family members
  • Work: Professional contacts and colleagues
  • Friends: Personal friends and acquaintances
  • Emergency: Important emergency contacts
  • School: Educational contacts and teachers

Search & Filter

Searching Contacts

  • Use the search box at the top of the contacts page
  • Search by name, organization, or phone number
  • Results update as you type

Filtering Options

  • Category Filter: Show contacts from specific categories
  • Status Filter: Show Important, Archived, or All contacts
  • Combined Filters: Use search and category filters together

Bulk Operations

Selecting Multiple Contacts

  1. Check the checkbox next to contact names
  2. Use "Select All" to choose all visible contacts
  3. Selected contacts show a blue highlight

Bulk Actions Available

  • Bulk Email: Send email to multiple contacts
  • Bulk Category Update: Add categories to multiple contacts
  • Bulk Status Update: Mark multiple contacts as important/archived

Duplicate Management

Finding Duplicates

  • Duplicate badges appear on contacts with similar names or phone numbers
  • Click on a contact with a duplicate badge to see options

Merging Duplicates

  1. Click on a contact with duplicate badge
  2. Click "Merge Duplicates"
  3. Select which contacts to merge
  4. Click "Merge Selected"
  5. The original contact keeps all information from merged contacts

Export & Backup

Exporting Contacts

  1. Click "Export Contacts" button
  2. Choose to export all contacts or selected contacts
  3. Download as CSV file
  4. Open in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app
Backup Strategy
  • Automatic: Firebase backs up your data automatically
  • Manual: Export contacts regularly as CSV
  • Multiple Devices: Access from any device with internet

Security Features

Access Control

  • Only authorized family members can access the app
  • Email verification required for all accounts
  • Secure authentication through Firebase

Data Protection

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Firebase security rules protect your data
  • No public access to your contact information

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Can't sign in?

  • Check your email and password
  • Make sure you're using the correct email address
  • Contact a family member if you need access

Contact not saving?

  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure all required fields are filled
  • Try refreshing the page

App not loading?

  • Clear your browser cache
  • Try a different browser
  • Check your internet connection

Mobile Usage

Mobile-Friendly Design

  • Responsive layout works on phones and tablets
  • Touch-friendly buttons and controls
  • Optimized for mobile browsers

Mobile Tips

  • Bookmark the app for easy access
  • Use landscape mode for better viewing
  • Enable notifications if available

Pro Tips

Efficient Contact Management

  1. Use categories consistently for easy filtering
  2. Add multiple phone numbers for important contacts
  3. Include notes for special instructions or preferences
  4. Mark emergency contacts as important
  5. Regularly update contact information

Organization Strategies

  • Create family-specific categories (e.g., "Mom's Side", "Dad's Side")
  • Use notes for birthdays, anniversaries, or special preferences
  • Mark frequently contacted people as important
  • Archive old contacts instead of deleting them

Quick Reference

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Ctrl/Cmd + F: Search contacts
  • Tab: Navigate between form fields
  • Enter: Submit forms
  • Escape: Close modals

Common Actions

  • Add Contact: Dashboard → "Add New Contact"
  • Search: Use search box on contacts page
  • Filter: Use category dropdown
  • Export: Dashboard → "Export Contacts"
  • Manage Users: Dropdown menu → "Manage Users"

You're All Set!

Your family contact management system is now ready to use. Remember to keep your password secure and contact family admin for help.
