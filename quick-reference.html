Quick Reference Card

Fast access to the most common actions and shortcuts

Quick Start

  1. Visit: https://sadjei78.github.io/ma-webiii
  2. Sign up with your email
  3. Wait for family approval
  4. Sign in and start managing contacts!

Most Used Actions

Add Contact

  • Dashboard → "Add New Contact"
  • Fill required fields (Name, Phone/Email)
  • Click "Save Contact"

Find Contact

  • Use search box (top of page)
  • Filter by category (dropdown)
  • Click contact card for details

Edit Contact

  • Click contact card → "Edit Contact"
  • Make changes
  • Click "Save Changes"

Bulk Actions

  • Check multiple contacts
  • Use "Bulk Email", "Bulk Update Categories", or "Bulk Update Status"

Categories

Family Work Friends Emergency School

Use these to organize your contacts effectively

Dashboard Cards

  • Click any card to see filtered contacts
  • Total Contacts → All contacts
  • Important → Highlighted contacts
  • Recent → Recently added
  • Category cards → Contacts in that category

Common Tasks

Add Family Member

  • Dropdown menu → "Manage Users"
  • Enter email → "Add User"

Export Contacts

  • Dashboard → "Export Contacts"
  • Download as CSV file

Manage Categories

  • Dropdown menu → "Manage Categories"
  • Add/delete categories as needed

Need Help?

  • Can't sign in? Check email/password
  • Contact not saving? Check internet connection
  • App not loading? Refresh page or try different browser
  • Still stuck? Contact family administrator

Mobile Tips

  • Works great on phones/tablets
  • Bookmark for easy access
  • Use landscape mode for better view
Pro Tips
  • Mark emergency contacts as important
  • Use categories consistently for organization
  • Export your data regularly as backup
  • Use search to quickly find any contact
